About Us

Shan Sons Engineering Works is a company dedicated to serving the nation with high-quality auto parts, textile parts, and tractor parts.

We have been in business since 1989. We have been serving our customers ever since. We started our business as a small unit. Then with the help of ALLAH ALMIGHTY, we have been prospering and now we have a state-of-the-art facility.

We are proud to be serving our customers and our nation with the dedication of the best quality parts, and timely deliveries.
